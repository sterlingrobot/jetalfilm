One Man's Journey
To Become One Man

Jason's mind first split to protect him from ongoing trauma when he was six. He's now 39 with 26 known alters. "Ethan" (8) lives in a castle with knights, trap doors, and big battles. "Peter" (6) worries that he has Alzheimers, but none of the "adults" listen to his concerns. And when "The Bullet" has control, Jason wakes up in the hospital after attempting suicide.

But Jason doesn't let life f*ck him. After living on the streets of Chicago, "The Boyfriend" followed a woman to LA. Now he's living in "Purgatory House" - a halfway house he desperately wants to move out of. With help from Dr. Paula, Jason is struggling to put himself back together so he can have a safe home for the first time in his life.
